Purpose of Cafe Sahabat

The purpose of Cafe Sahabat is to build a bridge of friendship between Korea and President University. It serves as a launchpad for student-led innovation and community engagement, aiming to create a shared growth model where students, the community, and the university can thrive together.

The Meaning of Sahabat

“Sahabat” means close friend or dear companion. It carries a sense of trust, loyalty, and emotional closeness like someone who's been with you through ups and downs.
